- Crystal Guess Game -

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Four crystals will be shown, each with a different value. Clicking on them will add points to the count.

To win, match your score to the random number, if you go over you will lose!

The values of the four crystals will be hidden from you until you click on them, and will change for each new game.
